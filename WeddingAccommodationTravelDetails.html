🛏️ Accommodation Booking

We have reserved rooms for our wedding guests directly at the venue.
You do not need to book independently — your room is already arranged as part of our guest list at a fixed wedding rate.
✅ The length of your stay has been confirmed based on your RSVP with us.

When checking in, please mention “Ewa & Jack’s Wedding” to confirm your room and rate.

  • Rate: 399 PLN per night for a double room, including breakfast
  • Payment: Guests are kindly asked to pay at check-in (not in advance)
  • Check-in: From 16:00 (4:00 PM)
  • Check-out: By 12:00 (12:00 PM)

🕐 Early check-in may be possible depending on availability — please call the reception ahead to inquire.

🧳 A luggage room will be available if you arrive before your room is ready.

🍽️ There is a lovely on-site restaurant open daily from 12:00 to 21:00.

🍳 Breakfast is served separately in the hotel each morning from 07:00 to 10:00.

🚗 Getting There & Parking

By Car: Free on-site parking is available.

From Kraków Airport (KRK): Approx. 15–20 minutes by taxi or Uber.
From Kraków City Center: Approx. 20-minute drive.
Public Transport: Bolt and Uber are the most convenient options.

🚕 The hotel reception can assist with booking taxis — just call or ask at the front desk.
